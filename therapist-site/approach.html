My Approach

Compassionate but direct. Reflective but practical.

I help clients connect emotional truth to daily behavior. Together we identify the deeper pattern beneath symptoms, then build practical shifts that hold up in relationships, work, and stress.

This work integrates relational depth, attachment awareness, and concrete strategy. You will be met with respect, challenged with care, and supported in making changes that feel real.

Session structure

  • Slow down and track what is happening beneath the surface
  • Clarify emotional themes and relational patterns
  • Practice more direct communication and boundaries
  • Integrate insight into actionable weekly steps
