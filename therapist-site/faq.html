FAQ

Common questions before getting started.

Do you provide virtual therapy in California?

Yes. Sessions are currently offered virtually for California residents.

How long are sessions?

Most ongoing sessions are 50 minutes. We can discuss cadence and frequency during consultation.

Do you work with men and veterans?

Yes. This is a core focus of my practice.

Do you offer emergency services?

No. For immediate crisis support, call 988 or your local emergency number.
