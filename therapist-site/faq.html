Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with men and veterans?

Yes. A significant part of my practice is dedicated to men and veterans seeking focused, emotionally honest therapy.

Do you offer virtual therapy in California?

Yes. I provide virtual therapy for residents of California.

What can I expect in the first session?

We’ll clarify what brings you in, map key stress patterns, and discuss how therapy can support your goals.

How long does therapy last?

Length depends on your goals and needs. Some clients seek short-term support; others engage in longer-term depth work.

Do you provide crisis services?

No. If you are in immediate danger or crisis, call 988 or emergency services right away.
