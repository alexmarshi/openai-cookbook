About

I help people reconnect with their inner life so their outer life can finally make sense.

I'm Alexander Marshi, AMFT. My style is warm, direct, and deeply engaged. I work especially well with men, veterans, and high-functioning adults who carry a lot of responsibility yet feel emotionally alone.

Clients often tell me they’ve spent years being competent while quietly struggling with anxiety, shame, and disconnection. Therapy with me is a place to be honest without posturing, and to build a life that feels aligned rather than performative.
