About Alexander

A therapist, guide, and reflective partner for meaningful change.

I’m Alexander Marshi, AMFT. My work is built for people who are thoughtful, responsible, and quietly carrying more than anyone sees. I bring warmth, emotional precision, and directness—so therapy feels both human and useful.

I specialize in helping men, veterans, and high-functioning adults move through anxiety, burnout, identity pressure, and relationship strain with deeper honesty and better internal alignment.

I won’t offer generic advice or rehearsed scripts. We’ll work at depth, then translate insight into practical shifts in how you communicate, decide, and live.
